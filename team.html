The Team


Our team consists of extremely passionate young minds who work on different aspects and fields which when assembled together, make i-Help what it is. The entire team is made up of overall coordinators and various verticals, which focus on the development of academic process, media outreach, human resources and academic projects, to name a few. Each year, the team is newly selected which helps us make new improvements. Along with the core team, our organisation comprises nearly 250 volunteers every year, who teach the children during weekly visits, work on modules, develop projects and carry out the operations. Together, we ensure that i-Help delivers the best of what it stands for.

Academic Vertical

All children have dreams, and the right to strive for them. But few have the means to achieve them. The Academic Vertical works to attain equality of opportunities by bridging the gap. We aim at making the children capable of achieving their dreams and reaching their goals. With the help of uniquely crafted booklets, worksheets and video modules, we provide them with a creative learning experience. We not only focus on their academic progress but also their wholesome development. By implementing engaging teaching methodologies, we ensure that they are able to learn step by step, and they are able to use the learnings in their everyday life. Our goal this year is to reach out to as many children as we can and help them discover and realise their true potential. We wish to enable them to contribute to society and future developments
